Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click "Register" in the navigation bar. Fill out the form with your name, email, and password. You'll be asked to select your role (Citizen, Municipal Officer, or Administrator).

What roles are available?
  • Citizen: Submit and track complaints
  • Municipal Officer: Manage assigned complaints by department
  • Administrator: Full system access and user management

How do I submit a complaint?

After logging in, go to your dashboard and click "Submit New Complaint". Fill out the form with:

  • Category (Potholes, Street Light, Garbage, etc.)
  • Description of the issue
  • Address and landmark
  • Priority level
  • Up to 5 photos (max 5MB each)
What categories are available?
  • Potholes & Road Damage
  • Street Light Issues
  • Garbage Collection
  • Water Supply Issues
  • Drainage Problems
  • Other Issues

How do I check my complaint status?

Visit your dashboard to see all your complaints. Click on any complaint to view detailed information, status updates, and assigned officer.

What do the status badges mean?
  • Submitted - Complaint received
  • In Progress - Being worked on
  • Resolved - Issue fixed
  • Rejected - Complaint denied
How long does resolution take?

Most complaints are resolved within 7-14 days, but complex issues may take longer. You'll receive status updates throughout the process.

How do I manage assigned complaints?

Your dashboard shows complaints assigned to your department. You can:

  • Update complaint status
  • Add notes and resolution details
  • View citizen-submitted photos
  • Filter complaints by status and priority
How are complaints assigned?

The system automatically assigns complaints to officers in the relevant department with the fewest active complaints.

What can I do as an administrator?
  • View system-wide statistics and analytics
  • Manage all users (create, edit, deactivate)
  • Generate reports with filters
  • View and manage all complaints
  • Assign roles and departments to users
How do I generate reports?

Go to the Reports section in your admin dashboard. You can filter by date range, status, category, and department, then export to CSV.

Photo upload isn't working

Ensure your photos are under 5MB each and in JPG, PNG, or GIF format. Try refreshing the page and attempting again.

I can't log in

Check that your email and password are correct. Passwords are case-sensitive. If you've forgotten your password, contact an administrator.

The page isn't loading

Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or try a different browser.

Mobile app?

This is a web-based system that works on all devices. For the best experience, use a modern web browser on your mobile device.

Tips & Tricks
  • Be specific: Include exact addresses and clear descriptions
  • Add photos: Visual evidence helps resolve issues faster
  • Check regularly: Monitor your dashboard for status updates
  • Use correct category: This ensures proper department assignment
  • Stay patient: Complex issues may take time to resolve
