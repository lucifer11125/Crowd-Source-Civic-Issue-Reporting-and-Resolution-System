How It Works

Simple 3-step process to report civic issues

1. Submit Complaint

Fill out our simple form with details about the issue, location, and attach photos if available.

2. Auto-Assignment

Your complaint is automatically assigned to the appropriate municipal department based on the issue type.

3. Track Progress

Monitor the status of your complaint in real-time and receive updates as the issue progresses toward resolution.
